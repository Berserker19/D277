About Tampa

Tampa is a dynamic and swiftly expanding metropolis situated on the western coast of the state, adjacent to the Gulf of Mexico. Tampa is renowned for its extensive historical background, broad cultural influences, and thriving economic growth. The city's vibrant culture and consistent sunshine make it appealing to both visitors and new residents.

  • Population: 408,510
  • Year Incorporated: 1855
  • Region: West Central Florida
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: Lower to the state average
